How it works

From the world's press to your pocket

📰

The world's major media

Reuters, AP, BBC, CNN, CNBC, FT, Al Jazeera, Times of India, Nikkei, and dozens more — a transparent, public source registry spanning every region, with state-affiliated outlets flagged.

🤖

AI distillation, honestly attributed

Every four hours, pipelines read the wires and write one clear paragraph per aspect — in our own words, always attributing the sources — plus the decision hooks: what this could influence.

📬

A briefing pack, delivered down

Your app downloads the latest briefing like a newspaper. Nothing about you travels up — reading history, interests, and everything personal stays on your device.

Your information never leaves your device.

You are in charge of your own.

  • No accounts. Subscriptions run through the App Store and Google Play — payment and identity stay with Apple and Google, never with us.
  • All storage is local. Preferences, history, everything — on your device only.
  • No tracking, no analytics. We hold zero user data. There is nothing to leak, sell, or subpoena.
  • One-way traffic. The only network activity is downloading the day's briefing — like a newspaper through the door.
